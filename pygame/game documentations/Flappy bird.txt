Story

You control a small bird trying to fly as far as possible through dangerous pipes. The bird must stay in the air and avoid crashing into obstacles. You can get high scores aswell.

 PC / NPC

Player Character (PC)
 A small flying bird
 Moves forward automatically
 Player controls when the bird jumps

Non-Player Characters (NPCs)
 Pipes (main obstacles)
 Ground and ceiling (death zones)

Goals
 Stay alive as long as possible
 Fly through gaps between pipes
 Earn the highest score

 Feedback
 Score increases when passing pipes
 Sound effects when jumping, scoring, or crashing
 Visual effects when hitting an obstacle
 Game Over screen showing score and best score

Challenging and Not Boring
 Increasing speed over time
 Smaller gaps between pipes
 Multiple levels with different themes
 Reward system:
   
   Coins for passing pipes
   Unlockable bird skins
   Achievements for high scores
